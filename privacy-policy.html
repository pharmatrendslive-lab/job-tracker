Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Job & Internship Tracker ("the App"), built by PharmaTrendsLive, handles your information. This app is a free tool provided for pharmacy students and job seekers to track their job and internship applications.

1. What data the App handles

When you use the App, you may enter the following information about your own job/internship applications: company name, job title, application status, dates, source, contact details of recruiters/referrals, compensation notes, resume version, and free-text comments.

2. Where your data is stored

Your data is not stored on any server operated by PharmaTrendsLive or any third party. When you sign in with your Google Account, the App creates a Google Sheet titled "My Job Tracker - PharmaTrendsLive" directly inside your own Google Drive. All information you enter is saved to that spreadsheet, which belongs to you.

If you choose not to sign in with Google, your data is instead stored locally within your own browser/device and is never transmitted anywhere.

3. What the developer can and cannot see

PharmaTrendsLive, as the developer of this App, cannot see, access, or collect the data you enter. There is no backend server collecting or storing user data. Each user's Google Sheet is private to their own Google account, exactly like any other file in their personal Drive.

4. Google Account permissions ("scopes") used

When you sign in with Google, the App requests the following permissions, and uses them only for the stated purpose:

  • Your email address & basic profile info — used only to show "Signed in as [you]" inside the App interface.
  • Google Sheets access — used to create and update the single spreadsheet that stores your application data, inside your own Drive.
  • Google Drive access (file-scoped only) — limited specifically to files created by this App. The App cannot see, browse, or access any other file in your Drive.

The App does not request, and never has access to, your emails, contacts, calendar, photos, or any other Google data beyond what is listed above.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your data with any third party. We do not use your data for advertising. There are no ads in this App.

6. Data deletion

Since your data lives in a Google Sheet inside your own Drive, you are always in full control of it. You may delete individual entries from within the App, or delete the entire Google Sheet directly from your Google Drive at any time. You may also revoke this App's access to your Google Account at any time via Google Account → Security → Third-party access.

7. Children's privacy

This App is intended for pharmacy students and job seekers and is not directed at children under 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this same page with an updated "Last updated" date.
