PharmaTrendsLive Job Tracker

Job & Internship Tracker

A free, mobile-friendly tool built for pharmacy students and job seekers to track every job and internship application in one place — without wrestling with a spreadsheet on a phone screen.

Open the Tracker →

What it does

📋 Track every application
Company, role, status, dates, contacts, and notes — all in one simple card view.
🔄 Auto-syncs to your Google Sheet
Sign in with Google and your entries save automatically to a private spreadsheet in your own Drive.
🔔 Follow-up reminders
Applications you haven't touched in 10+ days are automatically flagged.
📊 Live dashboard
See your total applications, interviews, offers, and response rate at a glance.

Who made this

This tool is built and maintained by PharmaTrendsLive, an education platform for B.Pharm, M.Pharm, and PharmD students in India, as a free resource for its community.

🔒 Your data is private to you. Nothing you enter is visible to PharmaTrendsLive or anyone else — it's saved directly into your own Google Drive. Read the full Privacy Policy.
